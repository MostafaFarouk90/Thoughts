The Journey of a Story

From ancient India to medieval Europe, the parable traveled along trade routes and through scholarly exchange, adapting to new cultures and languages while retaining its core message.

2nd-3rd Century CE

Origins in India. Earliest evidence in Buddhist reliefs.

6th-8th Century CE

Appears in Hindu (*Mahabharata*) and Jaina texts.

8th-12th Century CE

Transmitted via Persia, appears in the Islamic world (*Kalila wa Dimna*).

11th Century CE & Beyond

Reaches Christian Europe through the popular novel *Barlaam and Ioasaph*.
